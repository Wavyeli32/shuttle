Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I vote in the e-voting system?

To vote, go to the "Vote" page and follow the instructions provided. Enter your Voter ID, select your preferred candidate, and submit your vote.

2. What should I do if I encounter technical issues during the voting process?

If you experience technical difficulties, please contact our support team by visiting the "Contact Us" page. We are here to assist you.

Tips for Navigating the Website

  • Ensure your internet connection is stable before participating in the voting process.
  • Read instructions carefully on each page to avoid any errors.
  • Contact our support team for assistance if needed.
